About

StuyHacks is a student-run hackathon taking place in the heart of Manhattan. We seek to expose as many high school students as possible to the creativity and innovation that technology brings. To us, hackathons are more than friendly competitions - they're opportunities to meet the great inventors, creators, and thinkers of the future. Our job is the eliminate the negative connotations that come with hacking and the technology field, and to instead replace them with the idea that coding something anyone can do, even in a weekend. We want to help trailblaze a new generation diversity and enthusiasm for invention. Come join our movement.

Get ready, get set, get HACKING!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who's invited to Local Hack Day - Stuyvesant HS (aka StuyHacks)?

Everyone! Even though the hackathon is only for high schoolers, if you're in college or above, you can still apply be one of our amazing mentorsat this link!

What if I don't know how to program?

Don't worry! You're still invited to join us! We'll have mentors eagerly waiting to help you out with any questions you may have, to get you started off on the right foot, and to teach you new skills. There will also be workshops running all day, so definitely look out for those!

What am I allowed to make?

In short, ANYTHING! If you're planning on creating a hardware hack, feel free to bring your own hardware! Otherwise, you can use any APIs, development stacks, programming languages, or tools you'll need to make your project complete!

How much does it cost to participate?

Nothing! Attending Local Hack Day - Stuyvesant High School is completely free! (So why not sign up now for awesome swag, food, and a great time?)

What should I bring?

We'll provide the hardware, food, and Internet. All you need to bring is:

  • A laptop
  • Your phone
  • Chargers and extesnion cords
  • Power strips
  • Optional: Hardware!

Do I need to stay in the building all day?

We recommend staying inside District CoWork for the duration of the hackathon, although you are free to leave if you need to do so.

How many people can I have on my team?

The maximum team size is four. You can also go solo or work with a smaller team if you'd like! If you need help finding a team, we'll be running a team building event and pitch at the start of the hackathon, so keep your ears peeled!

How is Local Hack Day - Stuyvesant HS run?

Our hackathon is run by a group of nerdy and far-too-caffeinated high school students from Stuyvesant High School. Our team also runs StuyHacks, a 24-hour hackathon that will be taking place later this year. Keep an eye out for us during the hackathon, and we'll give you a sticker!

Wait, I still have more questions!

Don't worry, we'll be available during the hackathon to answer any questions you have - just ask anyone in a staff shirt for help! Otherwise, feel free to email us at hello@stuyhacks.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
